About a cute dog

Golden Retrievers are considered one of the cutest dog breeds due to their lustrous, soft, golden-toned coats, floppy ears, and, most notably, a "smiling" expression, says Wag!. These loyal and intelligent dogs are highly endearing, often displaying a gentle, affectionate, and, as shown in YouTube videos, a truly charming, caring, and protective nature towards their owners and other animals.
